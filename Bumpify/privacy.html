Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2025

← Back to Bumpify

This Privacy Policy explains how Bumpify ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot. By adding Bumpify to your server, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

Bumpify collects only the minimal data required to function:

  • Server ID: Used to associate your configuration and bump data with your Discord server.
  • Channel IDs: Stored to know where to send bump reminders and leaderboards.
  • User IDs: Recorded alongside bump events to build per-user bump counts and leaderboards.
  • Bump timestamps: Used to calculate cooldowns and send reminders at the right time.
  • Configuration settings: Custom messages, role reward IDs, and embed colors you set via /config.

We do not read, store, or process the content of any messages in your server beyond direct interactions with Bumpify slash commands.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to operate and improve Bumpify:

  • Tracking bump cooldowns and sending automated reminders.
  • Maintaining leaderboards and bump streak statistics.
  • Applying your server's configuration (channels, roles, messages).
  • Diagnosing bugs and improving bot reliability.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Retention

Your server's data is retained for as long as Bumpify is active in your server. If you remove Bumpify, you may request deletion of all associated data by contacting us through our support server. Data will be deleted within 30 days of a verified removal request.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security, and you use Bumpify at your own risk.

5. Third-Party Services

Bumpify operates through the Discord platform and is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy. We do not currently integrate with any other third-party data processors.

6. Children's Privacy

Bumpify is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child under 13 has used our bot, please contact us and we will delete the relevant data promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. Continued use of Bumpify after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, please join our support server and open a ticket.
